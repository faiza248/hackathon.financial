About Us

Free Financial Resources at your finger prints

According to Forbes women in America weren't allowed to open a bank account, build credit, or take out loans until 1974! Finance Chicks is about empowering women with knowledge about money and finance. Fostering financial independence and equality is what we aim to do. When women are informed about personal finance, budgeting, investing, and saving strategies, they gain the confidence and skills needed to make sound financial decisions that positively impact their lives and the lives of their families. Financial literacy equips women with the tools to navigate economic challenges, pursue career opportunities, and plan for their futures with greater certainty.

As we wanted to help woman learn more about finance we conducted our own survey. And we asked several women regarding how they felt about their financial knowledge.


40% of women we interviewed mentioned wanting advice on controlling spending habits.
